Quick Troubleshooting

Booking Not Showing?

  1. Check your email for confirmation.
  2. Log in to your account and refresh.
  3. If still missing, contact our support team.

Payment Issues

  1. Verify with your bank if payment is processed.
  2. Check your booking status after 10 minutes.
  3. Reach out with transaction ID for faster resolution.

Trip Cancellations

  1. Go to "My Bookings".
  2. Click "Cancel Trip" and choose reason.
  3. Refund will be processed within 3-5 working days.
